About Us

Founded in 2017 as an OyeSpace holding company, ScuareX is a web/app based Housing Society Management and Safety System that has established a firm foothold in Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Jaipur, Chandigarh, Indore, Kanpur, Lucknow, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kochi, Hyderabad and we are growing exponentially. Our perspective to provide a shielded environment for your home has been and will always be the driving force to deliver impeccable service and raise the bar consistently.

ScuareX is a dynamic conglomeration of multiple dimensions that allow a smooth and efficient management of a residential complex. Factors such as entry/exit of staff & visitors, guests, facilities, assets, day-to-day operations, patrolling can be managed and supervised systematically and digitally. Moreover, ScuareX plays its part in bringing the community together and engage the residents in a fun and productive manner.

All you have to do is get in touch with us, we will walk you through our range of products and deploy our services as per your convenience.
