About Us

A Bengaluru, India based company - ScuareX is a web and mobile app based Housing Society Safety Management and ERP Platform that has established a firm foothold in Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Jaipur, Chandigarh, Indore, Kanpur, Lucknow, Bengaluru, Chennai, Kochi, and Hyderabad. At ScuareX our aim is to provide you with a well shielded, transparent, congenial and fun community living experience.

ScuareX is a dynamic conglomeration of multiple dimensions that allow for smooth and efficient management of a residential complex. Functions such as entry/exit of staff & visitors and guest validation, patrolling, expense capture, invoices can be managed and supervised systematically and digitally. Moreover, ScuareX has native features like Walkie-Talkie, Drone patrolling, AI powered automatic invoicing & reminders to residents for dues, automatic payments to vendors etc., play an important part in reducing time required to manage mundane tasks but, also bring in a lot of transparency.

These values have been and will always be the driving force to deliver impeccable service and raise the bar consistently.
