Introducing OyeSafe - an easy-to-use application that facilitates with wide range of security functions such as:

Courier Delivery Management.

Is a breeze yet air tight - all delivery people need to publish their mobile numbers via Missed call on a defined number or by OTP validation. Delivery staff are photographed using Face Detection. Residents get instant notification with visitor photo. Resident not at home? Mark to leave courier with Guard, pick it up yourself from Gate by showing a QR code to Guard. So far managed more than 2 million deliveries efficiently.

Automatic Drone Patrolling.

High rise buildings, large land areas, public meeting areas and sensitive areas need critical attention and is achieved by Automatic Patrolling by our Advanced Drones with footage beamed in real-time to Security Guard’s mobile phone. Once patrolling way-path and frequency is defined Drones get Autonomous. Our Drones have patrolled and secured 3 million people and flew 40000 Kilometres

SOS.

Save Our Souls is truly a lifesaver - residents get to notify Security Guards whenever they need help. An extremely useful tool for the elderly. Guards get to view all details of the requester including the GPS location on Google Maps interface for easy navigation to provide help.Saved 237 lives so far

Walkie-Talkie

Is a key feature in OyeSafe for Security Guards and they love it. One device-multiple uses. Has reduced costs of running a full blown Walkie-Talkie both from a hardware and licensing perspectives by 30%

Video Analytics.

Makes CCTV cameras Virtual Security Guards by processing live footage from the CCTV to detect various security incidents and instantly alerts Security Guards with the video footage and GPS location to act on priority. Security incidents such as Break-ins, Fire, Smoke and Unattended items are automatically identified and alerted. Avoided property loss to the tune of Rs. 317 crores

Advanced Child Exit

Has helped parents be relieved of the constant thought of children getting outside the gate without being monitored and approved. Every Child (even with a Guardian) seeking to exit the premise is alerted to Parents via the App and only upon approval received at the Guard App they are let out. Has saved more than 20 child abductions so far.

Automatic Vehicle Number Recognition

Automatically differentiates between residents and visitors from their vehicle number plates - no RFID, no NFC expenses.

QR based Guest Invitation and Approvals

Guests need to welcome with warmth and not registers yet, need to validated thoroughly. Residents invite Guests and when the Guests arrive at the Gate they flash the QR code present in the invite sent by the Resident and the Guard just scans the QR code and welcomes with a smile instead of a visitor register. Resident receives an instant notification.

Over-Staying notification for vendors

Is a critical feature that ensure Delivery Vendors are exited on time and when they are not Security Guards get Automatic Alerts to monitor anybody over-staying and avoiding possible misuse of facilities and everything else other than delivery completion.

Biometric Staff Validation

All Service Providers who gain access to Residences such as Maids, Cooks, Drivers are critically validated using Fingerprint Biometric, Mobile Number, Photograph and a Government issued ID Card. Currently managing 14000 staff.
